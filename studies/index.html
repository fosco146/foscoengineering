Can PE Power Review perform complete, turn-key power system and arc flash studies?

Yes, in addition to our independent third-party auditing services, PE Power Review engineers complete power system analysis studies from the ground up. We build native engineering models, perform data collection field verification, map protective device coordination schemes, and deliver fully stamped engineering reports ready for construction execution.

What specific analysis capabilities are included in your full power system engineering studies?

Our comprehensive engineering capabilities deliver technical execution across all phases of system simulation design. Our scope of studies includes:

  • Short-Circuit Analysis: Calculating baseline system limits and checking equipment duty ratings against component interrupting capacity per ANSI/IEEE standards.
  • Overcurrent Protective Device Coordination: Isolating faults to the nearest localized protective device to completely mitigate nuisance main-breaker tripping.
  • Arc Flash Hazard Evaluation: Determining exact incident energy thresholds, outlining boundary borders, and engineering code-compliant warning labels under NFPA 70E metrics.
